Topology & Explanation

The Network Topology for the Company TimeLine Consultancy is based on the assumption that there are only 3 departments :-
  -> Human Resource Department.
  -> Accounting Department.
  -> IT Department.

Given that TimeLine Consultancy has an IP address 192.168.30.0/24. To maximise its use for the three different departments. We do subnetting on the IP address.
Calculations
8 bits: 128   64   32   16   8   4   2   1
Since we want to have 3 different networks, our IP becomes:- 192.168.30.0/26
Therefore, the 3 subnets are:
-192.168.30.64   255.255.255.192
-192.168.30.128   255.255.255.192
-192.168.30.192   255.255.255.192
